About Me

I chose Babson College because I was impressed by Babson's entrepreneurial mindset, especially its FME courses, and I wanted to learn how to be a successful entrepreneur at Babson. Babson is known for its specialized entrepreneurship courses, great diversity of international students, rich resources of connections and various opportunities for networking. I was attracted by these aspects of Babson and finally decided to choose Babson over other schools. After I graduate from Babson, I plan to pursue master degree in business, preferably in West Coast, and explore more into technology field by getting to know more people who work in this field. I want to work in this industry for a couple of years to build my own connections and hopefully can launch my own business related to artificial intelligence in a few years.

Humans can hold their breath underwater and undertake rudimentary locomotive swimming within weeks of birth, as an evolutionary response.
